Legends of Real Madrid

Honoring the greatest players in Real Madrid's history

Cristiano Ronaldo

One of the greatest footballers of all time, Ronaldo led Real Madrid to numerous titles, including four Champions League wins.

Raúl González

Raúl was a symbol of leadership and passion, scoring over 300 goals for Real Madrid and winning multiple La Liga titles.

Alfredo Di Stéfano

Considered one of the best players of the 20th century, Di Stéfano was instrumental in Real Madrid's dominance in the 1950s and 1960s.
