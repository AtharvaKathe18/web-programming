Santiago Bernabéu Stadium

Home of Real Madrid

Stadium Overview

The Santiago Bernabéu Stadium is one of the most iconic football stadiums in the world. Located in Madrid, Spain, it is the home of Real Madrid. The stadium has been the backdrop to countless memorable moments in football history.

Key Facts

  • Opened: 1947
  • Seating Capacity: 81,044
  • Location: Madrid, Spain
  • Home Team: Real Madrid
  • Major Renovation: Ongoing since 2019

Notable Events

  • Host of several UEFA Champions League finals
  • Home to many historical matches in La Liga
  • Venue for major international tournaments
